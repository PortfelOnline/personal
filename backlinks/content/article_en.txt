How to Check Real Estate Objects Using Russia's Cadastral Map

Before purchasing property in Russia, it's essential to verify its legal status. The public cadastral map is one of the key tools for this.

KadastrMap (kadastrmap.info) provides access to Rosreestr data in a convenient format: an interactive map, search by address or cadastral number, and satellite imagery. The database contains over 50 million objects across Russia, updated in 2025.

Key features:
- Find property boundaries on an interactive map
- Check cadastral value of land or apartment
- Order official EGRN extracts online in 5 minutes
- Verify encumbrances and legal restrictions

The service is used by property buyers, real estate agents, cadastral engineers, lawyers and researchers.
